CRITICAL — Furniture rendering style

Every single piece of furniture must be represented as a plain, simple rectangle. No exceptions.
Do not draw any internal detail, texture, pattern, cushion lines, pillow shapes, burner circles, faucet shapes, or any illustrative/iconic furniture symbol inside the rectangles.
A bed is a rectangle. A sofa is a rectangle. A toilet is a rectangle. A sink is a rectangle. A shower is a rectangle. A table is a rectangle. Everything is a rectangle.
The only thing that should appear on each furniture rectangle is its numeric ID label — nothing else.
Do not attempt to make furniture "look like" what it is. The legend/key handles identification. The rectangle is only a placeholder shape.
Rectangles should be filled with a single flat color or light fill to distinguish them from walls, with no gradients, no hatching, no line detail inside.
The rectangles must be properly scaled to the real-world dimensions of the furniture they represent.
